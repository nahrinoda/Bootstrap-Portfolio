About Me

I am a professional pirate hunter, and when I am not on a hunting mission, I am usually designing amazing jewelry using a special 3D program. I sell the blueprints to manufacturers who later 3D prints my creations and use these prints as their basic wax.

So basically, i am a creative pirate hunter, who likes to fix electrical ovans.
